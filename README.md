🔥 BURNBOOK — PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated: May 2026

Overview
Burnbook (“the App”) is a simple, anonymous expression platform where users can write short posts that automatically delete after 24 hours. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how your data is handled.
By using Burnbook, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect
Burnbook is designed to collect as little personal information as possible.
2.1 Information You Provide
Text you write inside the app (“Burns”)

Optional profile name (if you choose to create one)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information
Device type (iPhone model, OS version)

App version

Basic analytics (crashes, performance data)

We do not collect:

your real name

your phone number

your contacts

your photos

your location

your IP address (beyond what Apple may collect through the App Store)

How Your Data Is Used
We use your data only to:
operate the app

improve performance

fix bugs

prevent abuse or violations of our Terms

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

Data Storage & Deletion
4.1 Burnbook Posts (“Burns”)
All posts automatically delete 24 hours after creation.
Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

We do not archive or back up deleted posts.

4.2 Account Data (if applicable)
If you create an optional profile:

You may delete it at any time

Deleting your profile removes all associated data

Third‑Party Services
Burnbook may use:
Apple App Store services

Crash reporting tools (e.g., Apple’s built‑in diagnostics)

These services may collect anonymous device data to help improve app stability.

Children’s Privacy
Burnbook is not intended for children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Security
We use industry‑standard security practices to protect your data.
However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Changes to This Policy
